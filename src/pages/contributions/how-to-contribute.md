In the meantime, here are the current ways you can contribute:

- **Add an event:** submit your information through our [Add Event](/contributions/add-event/) page.
- **Add an organization:** start an organization request on our [Add Organization](/contributions/add-organization/) page.
- **Contribute directly to the website:** open a pull request on [GitHub](https://github.com/kaianolevine/wcsmn.com)
